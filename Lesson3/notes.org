** Regular Expressions
| special | example | match               | meaning                |
| *       | a*      | '', a, aa, aaa, ... | anystring              |
| ?       | a?      | '', a               | zero or one appearence |
| .       | .       | a, b, c, 7, !,...   | any single character   |
| ^       | ^b      | ba, bb, ...         | beginning of the line  |
| $       | a$      | ba, aa              | end of the line        |
| ''      | ''      | ''                  | empty string           |
| a       | a       | a                   | single character       |
